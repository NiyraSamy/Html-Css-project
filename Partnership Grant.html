                Request for Partnership Grant Application Africa Relief and Community Development (ARCD) was established to address the immediate and long-term needs of
                poverty-stricken communities in Africa by providing comprehensive relief and developmental aid.

                Criteria for Grant Requests: ARCD will consider grant requests to organizations that

                -are non-profit entities

                -support programs related to water and sanitation, orphan sponsorship, student sponsorship, food aid, and/or healthcare aid.

                -are working within West, South, East, and/or Central Africa

                -have been in operation for a minimum of 5 consecutive years, and can provide a a portfolio containing all completed and in-progress reports when submitting the
                application
